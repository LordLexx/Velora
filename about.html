Our History

Our Journey of Excellence in Dedicated Home Care

1

Where Our Mission Began

Velora Care Group was founded by a collective of seasoned local healthcare professionals who saw a critical gap in the community. Having spent years on the frontlines of nursing and personal care, our founders came together with a clear, unified mission: to move away from rushed, robotic care visits and establish a standard of domiciliary support rooted in deep dignity and clinical excellence.

2

Rooted in Local Expertise

Instead of building a traditional startup from scratch, we built Velora on the back of decades of collective experience right here in Lancashire. By uniting senior care coordinators, specialist community nurses, and empathetic support workers, we created a powerhouse team capable of delivering complex, tailored personal care that families in Preston can completely depend upon from day one.

3

Shaping the Future of Care

Today, we are fully locked into our commitment to become the standard-bearer for domiciliary care across the region. By maintaining a strict focus on robust continuity of care, keeping our teams localized, and listening closely to the families we serve, we ensure that every client retains their independence in the place they love most—their own home.
